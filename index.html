Horse Back Riding

About my hobby

Horse back riding is ancient! Horseback riding has no set start date, but rather goes back as far as man-kind was on the move. Horses have moved and shaped our entire world. They are commonly, and rightfully called "beasts of burnden."

Horses are used more many reasons: pleasure, work, and therapy! My favorite style of riding is the "western style" of riding. This name is derived from the western American way of riding when it began! Most commonly people were used to English. Western style was created and named for the type of saddles used for the sport or work! The saddle horns are used to pull and maneuver cattle, hold for speed events, and many other uses! Personally, I use my horses for rodeo events and trail riding! Barrel racing is my sport of choice. There are many things I love about the sport. Trail riding is an absolute joy! Its a way to connect with your animal and nature; what could possibly be more theraputic than that! Check out my "about page" for more reading on the sport and my reasonings for my favorite hobby!
