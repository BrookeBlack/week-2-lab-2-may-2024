Riding Horses

About my favorite hobby

Horse riding has long been my favorite activity, even before I actually knew how! I am a "horse girl" at heart! I grew up watching tons of trainers on tv and the rodeo channel! I was totally in awe over the companionship these riders had with their horses. I chose to grow up and emulate that in every way I could and bring my passion to the table. I started out with the same horse I actually still have to this day! I hope that my style of training can be something to be aspired of as well.
