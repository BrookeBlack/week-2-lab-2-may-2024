Riding Horses

About my favorite hobby

Horse riding has long been my favorite activity, even before I actually knew how! I am a "horse girl" at heart! I grew up watching tons of trainers on tv and the rodeo channel! I was totally in awe over the companionship these riders had with their horses. I chose to grow up and emulate that in every way I could and bring my passion to the table. I started out with the same horse I actually still have to this day! I hope that my style of training can be something to be aspired of as well by my children.

Intrested in horse riding or ownership/husbandry yourself? Here is a breif list of things you would need to get started!

  • horse trailer
  • vehicle to pull your trailer
  • halter with a lead rope
  • pasture space with secure fencing or boarding facility
  • saddle
  • saddle pad
  • bridle set up
  • turn out blanket
  • brush set
  • hoof pick

Now, of course this is a very breif list, and multiple factors and cost go towards wach one but these are the basics! There is lots of reading on the subject, just a "Google" away!


There are multipe western competitive events to sign up for once you and your horse are set up and have an established schooling! Here is a list of my top five favorties!

  1. barrel racing
  2. pole bending
  3. goat tying
  4. trail
  5. team roping
